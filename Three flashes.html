WCAG 2.3.1 – Three Flashes or Below Threshold

This page demonstrates visual flashing content that meets and fails WCAG requirements.

✅ Passing Example (≤ 3 flashes per second)

Why this PASSES:
  • Flashes only once per second
  • Does not exceed seizure risk thresholds
SAFE FLASH

❌ Failing Example (> 3 flashes per second)

Why this FAILS:
  • Flashes more than 3 times per second
  • Can trigger photosensitive seizures
DANGEROUS FLASH
WCAG Requirement (2.3.1):
Web content must not contain anything that flashes more than three times per second, unless the flash is below the general flash and red flash thresholds.
